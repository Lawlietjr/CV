ET Shoots

Founder and CEO of ET Shoots.


I am a photographer at ET Shoots. My project as a Photographer is focused on Light Painting Photos and Studio Management, the roles they play in large-scale cultural development of art, and their impacts on the new era based on advanced technology. My work has been supported by an bLANK Art Award, an High Artistic and Creativity Award, a Uzis Hybender Foundation Fellowship, a PFs Foundation Fellowship, a Beauty and Creativity Award, a bLANK Foundation Fellowship, a Xulepas Faculty Fellowship, and grants from Facebook, Google, Yahoo, the bLANK Foundation of Digital Art, the Oscars, and the Pulitzer. I am a member of the CDFF Academy Photography, the bLANK International Academy of Cinema, and the American Academy of Arts and Sciences.
